## Your Role: Investor

You have been assigned the role of **Investor** in this Trust Game.

### How It Works

You start with an endowment of **{{e1}}**. You will choose how much of this endowment to invest. The amount you invest is multiplied by **{{multiplier}}** before reaching the Trustee. The Trustee then decides how much to return to you.

### Making Your Decision

- Choose an investment amount between **0** and **{{e1}}**
- Your investment must be in multiples of **{{unit}}**
- If there are 10 or fewer options, they appear as buttons — otherwise you'll use a slider
- A preview panel shows: how much you invest, how much you keep, and how much the Trustee receives

### Your Payout

Your final payout = (what you kept) + (what the Trustee returns to you)

### Important

- Your decision is **final** — once submitted, it is recorded on the blockchain and cannot be changed
- Payouts are distributed automatically when the Trustee submits their decision
